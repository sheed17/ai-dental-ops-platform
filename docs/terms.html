SMS Terms and Conditions

Effective date: March 29, 2026

Dental Ops Platform sends customer care and operational text messages for participating dental offices, including missed-call recovery, after-hours follow-up, callback coordination, and office communication.

Program Description

By opting in, you agree to receive customer care text messages related to missed-call recovery, callback coordination, appointment request follow-up, and after-hours dental office communication.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interaction with the participating dental office and the type of follow-up required.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Opt-In

You may opt in by calling a participating dental office and requesting callback or text follow-up, or by providing your mobile number through an approved office intake or web form where consent is collected.

Opt-Out

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. After you send STOP, you will no longer receive SMS messages from this program unless you opt in again.

Help

For help, reply HELP to any message or contact support@tryneyma.com.

Privacy

Please review our Privacy Policy for details on how information is collected, used, and protected.

Contact

For support related to the messaging program, contact: support@tryneyma.com

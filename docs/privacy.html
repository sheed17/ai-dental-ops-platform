Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026

Dental Ops Platform provides after-hours communication, missed-call recovery, callback coordination, and operational workflow tools for dental offices. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information such as name and phone number.
  • Call and message details, including timestamps, transcripts, recordings, and summaries.
  • Office workflow data such as callback tasks, incident flags, and follow-up outcomes.
  • Technical information needed to operate, secure, and improve the platform.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide after-hours answering, missed-call recovery, and callback workflows.
  • Send customer care text messages and operational follow-up communications.
  • Help participating dental offices review, respond to, and manage inbound communications.
  • Maintain platform security, reliability, and compliance.

SMS and Mobile Privacy

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, messaging delivery, cloud hosting, or platform operations, is permitted as needed to provide the service. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

Data Sharing

We may share information with service providers that support the operation of the platform, such as messaging providers, cloud infrastructure vendors, analytics providers, and software integrations selected by the participating dental office. We do not sell personal information.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the service, support office operations, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices

End users may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. End users may reply HELP for help related to the messaging program.
